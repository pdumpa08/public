ABOUT

The Virtual Medicine Mission (VMM) Healthcare program is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving healthcare access in underserved
communities through the innovative use of telemedicine. Our mission is to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and patients by leveraging technology to deliver high-quality medical services remotely. We connect medical professionals and technology with patients in remote or underserved areas, providing consultations, diagnosis, and treatment options. Our dedicated team of healthcare providers is passionate about delivering compassionate care and making a positive impact on individuals and communities. We believe that together, we can make a difference in the healthcare systems of underserved communities, starting with Godo, Kenya.
